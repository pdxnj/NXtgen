About NextGen

My name is Elliot, and I'm a 19-year-old recent graduate expanding my home services business. Over the past few years, my friends and I have been going door-to-door offering reliable help with window cleaning, lawn care, and seasonal services — including holiday light installation, Christmas tree setup and takedown, and junk removal. Through our work, we've seen firsthand how much of a difference dependable local help can make. What started as a small neighborhood effort has grown into something that benefits both our team and our community. Our goal is to keep building our business while continuing to provide honest, quality service to the people who make our community great.
